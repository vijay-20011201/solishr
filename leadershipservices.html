Strategic & Confidential Leadership Recruitment

Leadership hiring is not the same as regular recruitment. It requires strategic planning, deep industry insight, high-level evaluation, and complete confidentiality. Solis follows a research-driven executive hiring methodology to identify leaders who can influence growth and long-term organizational success.

01

Leadership Role Definition & Organizational Mapping

We collaborate with founders, board members, and senior stakeholders to define leadership expectations, organizational gaps, cultural needs, and long-term strategic goals. This ensures clarity on the exact leadership persona required.

02

Market Intelligence & Executive Talent Mapping

Our team conducts deep market research including competitor leadership structures, talent availability, industry benchmarks, and leadership movement trends. This helps identify where the right leaders currently operate and what value they bring.

03

Confidential Executive Search & Outreach

Unlike normal hiring, executive search involves targeted headhunting, discreet networking, and direct engagement with high-performing leaders who are not actively searching for jobs. Absolute confidentiality is maintained throughout.

04

Leadership Evaluation & Strategic Assessment

Every shortlisted leader undergoes multi-dimensional evaluation including leadership style, transformation capability, strategic vision, P&L ownership experience, and organizational culture compatibility to ensure long-term impact.

05

Confidential CXO-Level Interview Coordination

We coordinate structured, discreet interview rounds with founders, board members, and senior management. This includes business-case evaluations, scenario-based assessments, and behavioral or psychometric profiling when required.

06

Offer Structuring, Negotiation & Executive Onboarding

We support both the organization and the leader through compensation structuring, ESOP planning, negotiation facilitation, transition management, and onboarding alignment to ensure a successful leadership integration.
